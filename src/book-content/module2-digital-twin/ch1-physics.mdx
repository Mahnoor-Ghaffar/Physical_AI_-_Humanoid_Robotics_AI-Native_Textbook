# Chapter 1: Physics in Digital Twins

## 1. Epic Overview
This chapter explores the fundamental principles of physics simulation within digital twins, focusing on how real-world physical behaviors are replicated in virtual environments like Gazebo and Unity. It covers topics such as rigid body dynamics, collision detection, and material properties, essential for creating accurate and interactive humanoid robot simulations.
