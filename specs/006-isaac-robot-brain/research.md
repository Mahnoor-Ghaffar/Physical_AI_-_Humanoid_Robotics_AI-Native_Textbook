## Phase 0: Outline & Research

### Research Tasks

1.  **Research NVIDIA Isaac Sim 5.1 (and any 2025 updates if released)**
    *   **Objective**: Understand the latest features and best practices for photorealism, synthetic data generation, USD asset utilization, OmniGraph workflows, and domain randomization within Isaac Sim 5.1.
    *   **Prioritized Sources**: developer.nvidia.com/isaac-sim, official Isaac Sim documentation.
    *   **Expected Output**: Key features, workflow examples, and performance considerations for each area.

2.  **Research Isaac ROS 3.2**
    *   **Objective**: Investigate Isaac ROS 3.2 capabilities for VSLAM, sensor fusion techniques, the usage and benefits of GEMs extensions, CUDA-accelerated nodes, and robust ROS 2 integration for hardware-accelerated perception.
    *   **Prioritized Sources**: developer.nvidia.com/isaac-ros, official Isaac ROS documentation.
    *   **Expected Output**: Details on VSLAM pipeline configuration, sensor fusion strategies, GEMs deployment, and performance optimization for edge hardware.

3.  **Research ROS 2 Kilted Kaiju (and any newer updates for 2025)**
    *   **Objective**: Identify key features, changes, and integration considerations of ROS 2 Kilted Kaiju relevant to Isaac ROS and Nav2.
    *   **Prioritized Sources**: ros.org, official ROS 2 documentation.
    *   **Expected Output**: Compatibility notes, new features impacting Isaac integration, and best practices for ROS 2 setup on Ubuntu 24.04.

4.  **Research Nav2 1.4.x for Bipedal Humanoids**
    *   **Objective**: Explore Nav2 1.4.x functionalities, with a specific focus on extensions or configurations suitable for bipedal locomotion, the role of behavior trees, costmap tuning for humanoid robots, and the development or adaptation of humanoid-specific navigation plugins.
    *   **Prioritized Sources**: ros.org Nav2 documentation, peer-reviewed papers on humanoid navigation.
    *   **Expected Output**: Configuration guidelines for bipedal Nav2, examples of humanoid behavior trees, and strategies for obstacle avoidance unique to bipedal robots.

5.  **Research Best Practices for APA Citations in Docusaurus MDX**
    *   **Objective**: Determine the most effective and compliant methods for integrating APA-style citations and reference lists within Docusaurus MDX content, ensuring proper formatting and automated generation if possible.
    *   **Prioritized Sources**: Docusaurus documentation, MDX formatting guides, APA style guides.
    *   **Expected Output**: Recommended MDX syntax for citations, potential Docusaurus plugins for reference management, and formatting guidelines.

6.  **Research Best Practices for WCAG AA Compliance for Docusaurus Sites**
    *   **Objective**: Identify critical accessibility guidelines (WCAG AA) relevant to Docusaurus websites and determine strategies for achieving compliance, especially concerning interactive components and embedded media.
    *   **Prioritized Sources**: WCAG guidelines, Docusaurus accessibility documentation, web accessibility best practices.
    *   **Expected Output**: Checklist of key WCAG AA requirements, Docusaurus theme/component adjustments for accessibility, and testing methodologies.

7.  **Research Integration Patterns for Interlinking Textbook Modules**
    *   **Objective**: Investigate effective strategies for creating seamless interlinking between Module 3 and related modules (e.g., Module 2 for simulation handoff, Module 4 for VLA integration), ensuring a coherent user journey and knowledge progression.
    *   **Prioritized Sources**: Docusaurus linking documentation, information architecture best practices.
    *   **Expected Output**: Recommended linking conventions, potential cross-module data sharing considerations, and strategies for maintaining navigation continuity.

8.  **Research Unitree H1 Integrations with Isaac Sim/ROS**
    *   **Objective**: Explore existing or potential integration methods for the Unitree H1 humanoid robot model within NVIDIA Isaac Sim and Isaac ROS, focusing on relevant assets, drivers, and simulation capabilities, assuming a generic humanoid example is also covered.
    *   **Prioritized Sources**: Unitree Robotics documentation, NVIDIA Isaac Sim forums/examples, ROS communities.
    *   **Expected Output**: Overview of H1 model availability, simulation setup, and specific integration challenges or solutions.
